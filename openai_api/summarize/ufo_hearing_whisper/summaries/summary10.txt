During the hearing on unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs), witnesses shared their personal experiences and discussed the lack of transparency and fear of retaliation associated with UAP encounters. They also suggested possible involvement of the US government in a disinformation campaign. Witnesses emphasized the importance of transparency, accountability, and the role of NASA and the scientific community in investigating UAPs. They called for a change in reporting culture and secure facilities to share classified information. Witnesses recommended comprehensive analysis, expert consultations, and obtaining radar and photographic evidence for further examination. The hearing concluded with appreciation for the work done and the possibility of making more information public in the future.
