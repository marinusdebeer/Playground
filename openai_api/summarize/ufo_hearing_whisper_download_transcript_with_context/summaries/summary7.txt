During a hearing on UAPs (Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena), witnesses expressed the need for a reporting system and centralized location to investigate UAP encounters. They were concerned about the potential threats to national security posed by UAPs and described maneuvers and behaviors that surpassed current technology. Witnesses also mentioned instances of retaliation for coming forward with their testimonies. Representatives emphasized the importance of government transparency and civilian involvement in understanding UAP encounters. There were also discussions about the 2019 classification guidelines for UAPs and the need for more transparency with the American people. Witnesses shared their experiences with UAP incidents and the challenges they faced in reporting them. Some witnesses mentioned the possibility of interdimensional phenomena and the disabling of sensor and collection systems by UAPs. The importance of changing the culture of reporting and providing secure facilities for discussing sensitive information was emphasized.
