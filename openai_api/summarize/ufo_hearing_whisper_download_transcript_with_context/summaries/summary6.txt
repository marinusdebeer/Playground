The witnesses, including Navy Commander David Fravor and Lieutenant Ryan Graves, discussed the need for transparency and a reporting system for Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) encounters during a hearing on UAPs. They expressed concerns about national security and described UAP maneuvers that defied current technology. The witnesses mentioned instances of retaliation for coming forward with their testimonies. Representatives emphasized the importance of government transparency and civilian involvement in understanding UAP encounters. The witnesses also discussed the involvement of defense contractor companies in UAP-related programs and activities. The need for a notification process and specific characteristics to be included in reports on UAP sightings were also mentioned. The witnesses highlighted the impact of UAPs on training ranges, and possible steps Congress can take to address this issue.
